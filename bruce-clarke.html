Bruce Clarke

St Matthews Anglican Church

Bruce Clarke is the senior minister at St Matthews Anglican Church in Manly, Australia. He came to Christ quite dramatically at 20, after years of trying to find himself through partying while at high school and university. A few years later, he went to Bible College and subsequently became an Anglican Minister. His passion is to teach people the Word of God as well as evangelize the vast numbers of unreached people who live down under in Australia.

In the year 2000, Bruce was the Australian Director for the first International Harvest Crusade with Greg Laurie (Harvest 2000) that was run in Wollongong, Australia. Through that connection, he has made lifelong friends within the Calvary Chapel movement. He is married to Cathey and has three adult children, with two of them now married.
