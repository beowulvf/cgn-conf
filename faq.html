Will there be childcare available if I bring my kids?

Childcare will not be provided. There will be a nursing mom’s room available.

Who can attend?

We are inviting all pastoral leadership and ministry worker teams, as well as those in training for ministry, along with their spouses.

What time does the conference start and end?

Registration opens at 10 AM on Monday, June 24. The first main session will begin at 2 PM. The conference concludes with the last session on Thursday, June 27, which will begin at 7 PM.

What airport is the closest? Do you provide rides from the airport to CCCM?

The two closest airports are LAX and SNA. Unfortunately, we are not able to provide rides from the airport; however, there are many services available for hire to and from these airports.

Something has come up, and I can't come to the conference anymore. Can I get a refund on my registration?

There will be a $25 cancellation fee per person should you need to cancel for any reason prior to 3 business days before the conference. No refunds will be given for cancellations occurring within 3 business days of the conference. There is an option to transfer your registration to another person.
