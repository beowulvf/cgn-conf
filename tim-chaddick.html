Tim Chaddick

Reality Church London

Tim Chaddick is the founding pastor of Reality LA, a rapidly growing church in the heart of Hollywood, California. He is also the lead pastor of Reality Church London, England. Reality LA and Reality Church London are part of the Reality family of churches, a movement committed to relational church planting and serving the broader body of Christ. Tim is the author of Better: How Jesus Satisfies the Search for Meaning.
