Árpi Horváth-Kávai

CCBCE

Árpi Horváth-Kávai his wife Andi were both involved in church planting and ministry for a decade before joining the staff at CCBCE in 2002.

Árpi has been teaching at CCBCE for many years and has been the Director of the Bible College for a few years. He and his family, love having people in their home, watching documentaries, and encouraging people to have thoughtful discussion.

Ask him about reviews on the latest movies and television series, any question about history, or quiz them on 80’s music and you might get some surprising responses.
